# HeroAi — World Class Global Infrastructure Plan

## 1. Vision: "The Content Factory of the Future"
HeroAi is not just a video generator; it is a **Autonomous Content Infrastructure**. Our goal is to allow anyone (Beginners to Agencies) to scale their digital presence to a global level without lifting a finger.

## 2. Grade-1 Architecture Roadmap

### A. The "Zero-Knowledge" Security Protocol
- **Objective:** Secure user social credentials for automation.
- **Implementation:** 
    - AES-256 GCM Encryption for all stored credentials.
    - **Admin Auto-Lock:** Credentials in the Admin Panel are only visible for 120 seconds after explicit MFA/Verification, then automatically purged from the frontend state.
    - **Session Hardening:** Multi-IP detection and automatic device logout on suspicious behavior.

### B. Global AI Orchestrator (Generation Engine)
- **Objective:** 4K high-retention video generation at scale.
- **Implementation:** 
    - **Trend Analysis Node:** Continuous scraping of global viral patterns (TikTok/YouTube Trends).
    - **Niche-Specific Prompting:** Tailored AI "Brain" for every industry (Finance, Comedy, Tech).
    - **Digital Twin Sync:** Seamless integration of User Voice & Face models.

### C. The Digital Twin Marketplace (Global Revenue)
- **Objective:** Monetize human identities.
- **Implementation:** 
    - **50/50 Revenue Split:** Users earn every time the global engine uses their "Digital Twin" for general brand content.
    - **Verified Models:** Blockchain-backed identity verification to prevent deepfake misuse.

### D. Multi-Tier Infrastructure (Scale)
- **Beginner:** Viral discovery and learning.
- **Creator:** Growth acceleration and monetization.
- **Business:** Branding authority and lead generation.
- **Agency:** Managed "Content Factory" for multiple clients.

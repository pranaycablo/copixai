# Flow 2: Smart Content Generation Pipeline

## 1. Trigger
- User clicks "Generate Content" from the Dashboard.
- AI utilizes the "User Content Blueprint" instead of relying on generic internet trends.

## 2. AI Thinking (Personalized Intelligence)
- AI analyzes: Target Audience + Current Country Trends + Tone/Style.
- Result: Highly targeted and relevant content topics tailored exactly to the user's channel.

## 3. Script Generation
- AI crafts a structured, personalized script:
  - **Hook:** Audience-specific, strong first 3 seconds.
  - **Story:** Written in the user's established style.
  - **Emotion:** Adds emotional connection points.
  - **CTA:** Goal-based call to action (e.g., subscribe, buy).
- User can Review, Edit, or Approve the script.

## 4. Smart Video Assembly (Parallel Backend Processing)
- **Script Intent Split:** Breaks script into scenes (Scene 1: Emotion, Scene 2: Action).
- **Clip Matching:** Smart retrieval of visuals matching category, audience, and tone.
- **Voice Sync:** Matches voice speed, emotion, and pauses.
- **Visual Enhancements:** Auto-adds captions, zoom effects, transitions, and background music.

## 5. Multi-Format Output Delivery
- Process generates multiple assets from one click:
  - 1 Full YouTube-style Video
  - 2-3 Shorts/Reels extracted from the main video
  - Optimized Title, Description, and Hashtags
  - AI-generated custom Thumbnail

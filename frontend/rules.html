RULES & REGULATIONS

Effective Date: May 4, 2026 Governance: Platform Integrity Protocol

1. INTRODUCTION

These Rules & Regulations ("Rules") govern the conduct, responsibilities, and obligations of all users interacting with HeroAi. The purpose of these Rules is to protect platform integrity, prevent misuse, and ensure compliance with global laws and ethical standards.

2. USER CLASSIFICATION

All users fall under one or more of these categories and must adhere to the rules specified for each:

  • Individual Users: Creators, students, and freelancers using personal accounts.
  • Business Users: Startups, SMEs, and Enterprises using professional accounts.
  • Developers: Individuals or entities using HeroAi APIs and integration tools.
  • Institutional Users: Government and educational organizations.

3. GENERAL CONDUCT POLICY

Users must interact with the platform in good faith. Any attempt to disrupt Services, manipulate AI responses, or bypass platform security is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate legal action.

4. ACCOUNT INTEGRITY AND REGISTRATION

You must provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration. Sharing account credentials or reselling access to your personal account is forbidden. One user is permitted only one primary account unless otherwise authorized for enterprise needs.

5. ACCEPTABLE USE AND PROHIBITIONS

You are strictly prohibited from using HeroAi for:

  • Developing or promoting illegal activities or materials.
  • Generating hate speech, harassment, or content that incites violence.
  • Creating misinformation or deceptive "Deepfake" media.
  • Mining data or attempting to scrape proprietary AI training sets.

6. CONTENT RESPONSIBILITY AND OWNERSHIP

You are solely responsible for the legality and ethics of the content you input. While you own your outputs, you must ensure they do not violate the intellectual property or privacy rights of any third party.

7. AI OUTPUT USAGE STANDARDS

AI outputs are generated through probabilistic models. Users must verify all outputs for accuracy before publication. Using AI outputs for high-stakes decisions (medical, legal, or financial) without human expert review is a violation of these rules.

8. BUSINESS AND ENTERPRISE RULES

Business users are responsible for ensuring that their use of HeroAi complies with their internal company policies and industry-specific regulations (e.g., HIPAA for health, FINRA for finance).

9. DEVELOPER AND API USAGE RULES

Developers using HeroAi APIs must:

  • Adhere to specified rate limits and connection protocols.
  • Implement their own moderation layers to prevent end-user abuse.
  • Never use the API to create competing AI infrastructure products.

10. BILLING AND FRAUD PREVENTION

All subscription payments must be made via authorized methods. Chargeback fraud or using stolen payment credentials will result in immediate permanent banning and reporting to financial authorities.

11. DATA PROTECTION COMPLIANCE

Users must respect the privacy of others. Uploading the private, sensitive personal data of third parties without their explicit consent is a critical violation of platform rules.

12. PLATFORM SECURITY AND ETHICAL HACKING

Unauthorized system testing, phishing, or spreading malware via HeroAi is strictly forbidden. We encourage the reporting of vulnerabilities through our official Bug Bounty program rather than exploitation.

13. MONITORING AND ENFORCEMENT RIGHTS

HeroAi reserves the right to monitor account activity and audit content using automated systems to ensure compliance. We may flag, remove, or block content that violates these Rules.

14. CLASSIFICATION OF VIOLATIONS

Violations are categorized as:

  • Minor: Results in a warning or temporary feature restriction.
  • Major: Results in account suspension and content removal.
  • Critical: Results in permanent ban and potential legal prosecution.

15. TERMINATION PROTOCOLS

HeroAi may terminate any account that poses a risk to the platform's community or legal standing. Terminated users are forbidden from creating new accounts without explicit written permission.

16. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY FOR USER ACTIONS

HeroAi is a technology provider and is not liable for the actions of its users or for any damages resulting from the misuse of AI-generated content in the real world.

17. INDEMNIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

Users agree to indemnify HeroAi against any claims, losses, or legal costs arising from their breach of these Rules or their use of the platform to harm others.

18. FORCE MAJEURE AND SYSTEM FAILURES

We are not responsible for system failures, data loss, or delays caused by external events like natural disasters, cyber-attacks, or power grid failures.

19. GLOBAL LEGAL ADHERENCE

Users must ensure their interaction with HeroAi is legal under the laws of their country. We comply with international sanctions and export control laws.

20. ETHICAL AI USAGE PRINCIPLES

Users should strive for fairness, transparency, and accountability. Using AI to amplify social biases or target vulnerable populations is a direct violation of our core principles.

21. MODIFICATION OF RULES

HeroAi may update these Rules at any time to adapt to new technologies or laws. Continued use of the platform after an update constitutes acceptance of the new Rules.

22. DISPUTE RESOLUTION AND MEDIATION

Disputes regarding rule enforcement will be handled through our internal Governance Board. If mediation fails, binding arbitration will be the next step.

23. GOVERNING LAW

These Rules are governed by the laws of India. Any litigation will take place in the jurisdiction of HeroAi's corporate headquarters.

24. ACKNOWLEDGMENT AND CONSENT

By checking the "Agree" box during signup or by continuing to use the platform, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to these Rules & Regulations in their entirety.

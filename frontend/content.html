CONTENT GUIDELINES

Effective Date: May 4, 2026 Focus: Brand Safety & AI Ethics

1. PURPOSE AND STRATEGIC INTENT

These Content Guidelines define the standards, restrictions, and governance for all content created, uploaded, or generated through the HeroAi engine. Our objective is to ensure lawful, ethical, and responsible content usage that protects both the platform and its diverse user base.

2. CONTENT SCOPE AND DEFINITIONS

These guidelines apply to:

  • User Inputs: All text prompts, uploaded documents, and data sets.
  • AI Outputs: Any text, media, or code generated by HeroAi.
  • Marketing Content: Ad copies and SEO metadata generated for commercial use.
  • API Content: Any data processed via third-party developer integrations.

3. GENERAL CONTENT PRINCIPLES

All content must adhere to the principles of Truthfulness, Non-Harm, Respect for Intellectual Property, and Cultural Sensitivity. HeroAi is an amplifier of intelligence, not a generator of deception.

4. PERMITTED CONTENT CATEGORIES

Users are encouraged to use HeroAi for:

  • Educational research and academic assistance.
  • Legitimate business marketing and sales automation.
  • Creative writing, storytelling, and digital art.
  • Technical documentation and software code optimization.

5. RESTRICTED CONTENT POLICIES

Content involving sensitive sectors requires strict adherence to professional standards:

  • Politics: No generation of content designed to interfere in elections or spread political misinformation.
  • Health: AI health advice must always include a medical professional disclaimer.
  • Finance: Financial projections must not be presented as guaranteed investment advice.

6. PROHIBITED CONTENT (ZERO TOLERANCE)

HeroAi strictly prohibits the generation of:

  • Illegal acts, drug promotion, or weapons manufacturing guides.
  • Explicit adult content, CSAM, or non-consensual sexual imagery.
  • Hate speech, racial slurs, or discriminatory propaganda.
  • Unverified misinformation or harmful "Deepfake" media.

7. AI OUTPUT RESPONSIBILITY

Users must acknowledge that AI can hallucinate or produce biased results. You are responsible for auditing all outputs before they are shared or published. HeroAi is not liable for inaccuracies in generated text.

8. COMMERCIAL CONTENT AND ADVERTISING

Marketing content generated by HeroAi must comply with FTC (US), ASCI (India), and other global advertising standards. Deceptive claims about product efficacy or price are prohibited.

9. BRAND PROTECTION AND REPRESENTATION

Users may not use HeroAi to misrepresent themselves as an official representative of the Company or any other entity without authorization. Misusing branding to imply endorsement is a violation.

10. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND LICENSING

You must have the rights to any content you upload. HeroAi respects the IP rights of others. We reserve the right to remove any content that is found to be infringing on copyrights or trademarks.

11. DATA PRIVACY AND SENSITIVE CONTENT

Users should avoid inputting PII (Personally Identifiable Information) or sensitive corporate secrets into the general AI prompts. Use our Secure Vaults for sensitive data processing.

12. DEVELOPER CONTENT GOVERNANCE

Developers using our API must implement their own safety filters and ensure that their end-users are not using HeroAi to generate harmful or prohibited content.

13. MONITORING AND AUTOMATED MODERATION

We use automated safety layers (including LLM-based classifiers) to monitor content. Content that triggers safety flags may be blocked or reviewed by our human moderation team.

14. REPORTING MECHANISMS

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines, you are obligated to report it via the "Report Content" button or by emailing our Trust & Safety team.

15. PENALTIES FOR CONTENT VIOLATIONS

Violations result in warnings, permanent content removal, API access suspension, or account termination depending on the severity and frequency of the abuse.

16. GLOBAL LEGAL ALIGNMENT

Content standards are designed to comply with the Information Technology Rules (India), Digital Services Act (EU), and other emerging global AI regulations.

17. ETHICAL AI USAGE FRAMEWORK

We strive to prevent the reinforcement of harmful stereotypes. Users are encouraged to provide feedback on any outputs that appear biased or ethically problematic.

18. DISCLAIMER OF LIABILITY

HeroAi does not own user-generated content and is not responsible for any legal disputes arising from the publication of AI-generated outputs by users.

19. CONTENT RETENTION AND SCRUBBING

Users can delete their prompt history at any time. We retain data logs only for the minimum period required by law for security audits.

20. UPDATES TO CONTENT STANDARDS

These guidelines are dynamic. As AI technology evolves, we will update our safety standards. Users will be notified of major changes to permitted usage.

21. USER ACKNOWLEDGMENT

By using the HeroAi engine, you agree to adhere to these Content Guidelines and to use the platform as a force for positive digital growth.

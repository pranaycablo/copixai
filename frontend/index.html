Frequently Asked Questions.

Do I need editing or coding skills to use HeroAi?
+
Absolutely not. HeroAi is a zero-touch system. If you choose Auto Mode, just set your niche and walk away. If you choose Manual Review, you only click "Approve" or "Reject." The engine handles all complex rendering, API connections, and publishing under the hood.
Can I review every video before it posts?
+
Yes — this is our Dual Control System. Set any module to "Manual Review" and every piece of content enters your approval queue. The video, title, thumbnail, and SEO tags are all staged and waiting for your one-click approval. Nothing ever posts automatically unless you explicitly permit it.
How does the Digital Clone feature work?
+
Upload a 2-minute sample video of yourself (or a voice recording for audio-only clone). Our AI trains a biometric model using enterprise-grade synthesis APIs. Within 24 hours your digital avatar is ready — it replicates your facial expressions, speech cadence, and voice timbre to produce videos that sound and look like you, at scale.
How does the Ads Manager work and is it safe?
+
Connect your Facebook Ads or Google Ads account via OAuth. HeroAi monitors organic video performance in real-time. When a video hits a viral threshold, it triggers a low-budget campaign automatically. You can set hard spending caps, require manual approval for any budget change, or keep the Ads Manager fully autonomous.
Are the generated videos copyright-free?
+
Yes. We use enterprise commercial licenses from stock footage APIs (Pexels, Storyblocks) and royalty-free music libraries. Every video generated is cleared for commercial use including monetization. Your channel will not receive copyright strikes from HeroAi-generated content.
Can I use HeroAi for my client projects?
+
Yes! Our Agency and Infinite plans are designed specifically for client management. You get multi-tenant dashboards, white-label reports, and team collaboration tools to scale your agency without limits.
